### Long Description (1500 characters max)

Hotel Del Live Cam brings the beautiful beaches of Coronado, California to your living room with live HD webcam streams from the world-famous Hotel del Coronado.

**Features:**
• Two live camera feeds — Beach Camera South and Beach Camera North — available 24/7
• Full HD (1920×1080) streaming with HLS adaptive bitrate
• Clean, minimal interface with an auto-hiding header that disappears after 5 seconds
• Deep linking support — launch directly to a specific camera from the Roku search or external apps
• Automatic stream recovery — if a feed drops, the app retries within seconds
• Exit confirmation dialog to prevent accidental channel closure

**Why You'll Love It:**
Whether you're a former visitor longing for the Coronado surf, planning your next trip, or just looking for a relaxing live backdrop, Hotel Del Live Cam delivers a window to one of California's most beautiful beaches — right on your TV.

**Privacy First:**
This channel collects no personal data. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics. The video streams are provided by HDOnTap and are subject to their availability. See our privacy policy for full details.

Free forever. No ads. No in-app purchases.

## Deep Linking Parameters

| contentId | mediaType | Behavior |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| `south` or `0` | `live` | Launches Beach Camera South |
| `north` or `1` | `live` | Launches Beach Camera North |
| _(empty)_ | — | Launches default (South camera) |
