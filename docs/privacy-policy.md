# Privacy Policy

**Hotel Del Live Cam** — Roku Channel  
**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

---

## Overview

Hotel Del Live Cam is a free Roku channel that displays publicly available live webcam streams from the Hotel del Coronado beach in Coronado, California. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information is (and is not) collected when you use this channel.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

Hotel Del Live Cam does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your Roku device ID or account information
- Your location data
- Your viewing history or usage patterns
- Any payment or billing information
- Any cookies or tracking identifiers

---

## Information Collected by Third Parties

### Roku Platform

This channel runs on the Roku platform. Roku, Inc. may collect device and usage data as described in the [Roku Privacy Policy](https://www.roku.com/en-us/legal/privacy-policy). We have no control over and no access to any data Roku collects.

### HDOnTap Streaming Service

Live video streams are delivered through the HDOnTap platform on behalf of Hotel del Coronado. When your Roku device requests a stream, a standard HTTP request is made to HDOnTap servers. HDOnTap may log your device's IP address as part of normal server operations. We do not receive or store these logs. Please refer to [HDOnTap's privacy practices](https://www.hdontap.com) for more information.

---

## Children's Privacy

This channel does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The channel displays only publicly available beach webcam footage and contains no interactive features, accounts, or data entry.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the channel after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
